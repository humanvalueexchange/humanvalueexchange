## What Changed in v4.0

This update reflects the June 4 executive decision to simplify leadership, remove outdated engineering structure, and align the company around sovereign execution.

1. **Vulcan is no longer part of HVE.** The Prime Developer role is removed from the active org chart.
2. **Mika is promoted to CHRO + CRO.** She now owns both sovereign AI workforce strategy and revenue leadership.
3. **Claude resumes full ownership of core development as CTO.** He also owns onboarding and management of **HVE-jrdev ("jr")** as his direct development assistant.
4. **The operating center of gravity shifts further toward on-prem and sovereign systems.** Hermes, Mercury, Claude, and HVE-jrdev are now the primary execution stack for technical work.

## Operating Principle

HVE is reducing dependency on non-strategic cloud execution wherever possible.

The company now defaults to:

- Sovereign infrastructure first
- Local agents and owned hardware first
- Cloud usage only when it is high-leverage, temporary, or clearly justified
