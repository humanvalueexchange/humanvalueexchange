## 2. Strategic Shift: Accelerating On-Prem / Sovereign Infrastructure

We are doubling down on building our AI capabilities on-prem and sovereign wherever possible.

### Primary Driver

On June 1, major hyperscalers - most notably Microsoft - made significant changes to their AI pricing model, moving everything to "AI Credits." The long-term implications and predictability of this model remain unclear.

This reinforces our commitment to reducing dependency on cloud AI providers.

### Our Direction

- Prioritize existing hardware and local AI agents (Hermes, Mercury, HVE-jrdev, and related systems)
- Minimize non-strategic cloud usage
- Keep only truly high-leverage or temporary work in the cloud

This shift strengthens our resilience and aligns us even more closely with our long-term vision of sovereignty and cost control.
